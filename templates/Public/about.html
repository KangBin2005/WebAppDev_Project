About Us

Our Vision

Inclusive society. Enabled lives.

SG Enable, the focal agency for disability and inclusion in Singapore, enables persons with disabilities to live, learn, work, and play in an inclusive society. We create equitable opportunities through thought leadership, sustainable social innovation, and impactful partnership. Guided by our core values—professionalism, innovation, and empathy—we envision a more inclusive Singapore and enabled lives.

Established by the Ministry of Social and Family Development in 2013, we are a registered charity and an Institution of a Public Character.

Our Mission

SG Enable creates equitable opportunities for persons with disabilities to aspire and fulfil their potential—through thought leadership, sustainable social innovation, and impactful partnership.

Our Core Values

Professionalism

We are honest, respectful, and continually developing expertise so that we remain a trusted first-stop for disability inclusion in Singapore.

Innovation

We are open to new ideas, bold in our initiatives, and always seeking improvement for lasting impact.

Empathy

We listen actively, show care, and value each individual to build trusted, inclusive partnerships.
